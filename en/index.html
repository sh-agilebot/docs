ExtensionAgilebot Extension SDK

The Extension System is a secondary development platform provided by Agilebot Robotics. It allows you to focus on your own business scenarios and break free from the limitations of traditional, fixed-function robotics. By leveraging the modular components and interfaces offered by the platform—such as motion control commands, register access interfaces, and UI interaction components—you can quickly build your own application framework.
You can also access the code samples, guides, and other reference materials you need to accelerate and improve the quality of your extension development.
